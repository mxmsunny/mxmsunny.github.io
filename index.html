About Me

I am Xiangmin Mo, but I also go by Sunny. Currently, I am an undergraduate student at City University of New York Hunter College, pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Chinese and a minor in Mathematics. I am passionate about frontend development, and I am always trying to find new things to learn and improve myself as a coder and as a person.


Going more in detail, I also a student of Hunter College's Chinese Flagship Program, which is a rigorous undergraduate program of study in Chinese language and culture that aims to enable highly-motivated undergraduate students to become global professionals. Under this program, I had the opportunity to study abroad in Taipei at National Taiwan University from June 2018 to August 2018 to study the Chinese language and culture. I am currently a student learning Web Development at CUNY Tech Prep, and I am virtually attending Chinese classes offered by my Chinese program.


I first became interested in Computer Science after being introduced to video games, specifically Pokémon and MapleStory. These two games were a huge inspiration, and it pushed me to study Computer Science in college. It was difficult learning Computer Science because I did not have any prior knowledge. Although it was an difficult journey, I became more passionate about computer science after I took an Open Source Software Development course at my college. I learned how to contribute to projects, and I learned how fun it is to collaborate with others. I was able to give back to companies that have helped me and others. It helped me open up from my shell, and I will always be thankful for those that I have met along the way.


I like learning new technologies, exploring new places, drawing digitally and on paper, collaborating, teaching, hiking, reading, and gaming! I use Procreate for digital art , and you can find my art here.
